Ingredients:
 - 1/2 cup milk 
 - 1/2 cup water 
 - sugar 2 teaspoon (to taste)
 - one full teaspoon tea
 - one ounce of ginger

Directions:
 1. In a pan start warming the water and slowly pour milk in it.
 2. After it starts boiling add sugar and tea and give it few boils. Pour the ginger and again give it some boils.
 3. Your ginger milk tea is ready.
